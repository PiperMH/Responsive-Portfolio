About Me

I graduated from Smith College in May of 2016 with a BA degree. My major was in Studio Art and my minor was in Economics. At Smith, I pursued coursework in Photography, Visual Studies, and Digital Media as well as business studies such as Sports Economics. I am proficient in Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign. I work well with people and my natural competitiveness drives me to do my best, whatever the task. I am good natured and enjoy challenges and learning opportunities.

While at Smith, I was a 4-year varsity rower in the 1V open eight. As a freshman, I was named Rookie of the Year across all athletic teams at Smith. As a senior I was named Varsity Team Captain. Also as a senior I was named a Pocock All American Athlete (Division III). I was Cutter House President for two years. I have proven leadership abilities, excellent time management skills and experience with project management.
